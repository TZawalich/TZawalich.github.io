Hey, I'm Trevor.

I'm a Front-End/Full-Stack Developer based in the Greater Vancouver Area.

After graduating from SFU and BCIT, I've spent the last few years working in both corporate and freelance development environments. I like to keep up to date on the various tech-stacks I work with through extra-curricular study, and make it a point to integrate whatever I learn into my personal skillset.

These days I spend a lot of my time designing and developing client-focused web applications utilizing HTML, CSS/SASS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, Express, PHP, Python, and more. (Check out my LinkedIn profile for a more robust list of the tech and frameworks I've used in projects).

When I'm not skittering around the web building stuff, I like reading sci-fi and medieval lit, checking out indie games, hitting the gym, listening to metal, and exploring the city.

If you want to collaborate, ask questions, or just chat, hit me up in my Contact Section and we can talk.
